White Space collapsing

When the browser comes across two or more spaces next to each other, it only displays one space. Similarly if it comes across a line break, it treats that as a single space too. This is known as white space collapsing.

Strong & Emphasis

Strong

The use of the strong element indicates that its content has strong importance.

The Word "Strong" is strong.

For example: Beware of Pocket Pickers.

Emphasis

The em element indicates emphasis that subtly changes the meaning of a sentence.

For example: My name is Shahzaib.

Logic is good for effective Programming.

Qoutes

Simple Qoute " "

The q element is used for shorter quotes that sit within a paragraph.

For example: Where there is a will there's a way.

BlockQoute

The blockquote element is used for longer quotes that take up an entire paragraph.

Allama iqbal said:

Indeed, in view of its function, religion stands in greater need of a rational foundation of its ultimate principles than even the dogmas of science.

Insert and Delete

The element can be used to show content that has been inserted into a document, while the element can show text that has been deleted from it.

The Price of this item is 20$ 15$.

s element

The s element indicates something that is no longer accurate or relevant (but that should not be deleted).

The price of this item was 240$ but now its price is only 200$.
